WATCH TO KNOW

SatyaPrem Ki Katha movie



SatyaPrem Ki Katha is a romantic musical jointly produced under the banner of Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment and Namah Pictures. Director Sameer Vidwans known for his work on the National Award Winning film Anandi Gopal comes onboard to direct this romantic drama. The films score is handled by esteemed Bollywood singer Sunidhi Chauhan's husband Hitesh Sonik.

Bollywood's leading celebrities Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani star in the titular roles of SatyaPrem and Katha. This is the first time the duo will be seen sharing the screen together after their blockbuster appearance in Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2.
